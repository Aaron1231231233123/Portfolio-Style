Interaction principles

  • High feedback, low friction
  • Readable at a glance
  • Playful, never chaotic
  • Keyboard & screen-reader friendly

These principles drive every animation and microinteraction, from orbiting planets to how cards expand when you explore them.

Tech stack

100% HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — using browser APIs for motion, theming, and state, without external frameworks.

The implementation is intentionally transparent: easy to inspect, remix, and extend if you want to build your own interactive space.
